Frequently Asked Questions

What is the AI Justice ADHIKAAR Chatbot?

The AI Justice ADHIKAAR Chatbot is an automated system designed to provide legal information, guide users through legal processes, and help them understand their rights using artificial intelligence.

How can I use the AI Justice Chatbot?

You can interact with the AI Justice Chatbot directly on our website. Simply type your legal questions or concerns, and the chatbot will provide you with relevant information and guidance.

Is the information provided by the chatbot legally binding?

No, the information provided by the AI Justice Chatbot is not totally legally binding. It is meant for informational purposes only and should not be considered as legal advice. For specific legal issues, it is recommended to consult with a licensed attorney by taking membership provided on our website.

Does your AI Chatbot support Multilingual feature?

The AI Justice Chatbot currently supports multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Arabic, and more. We continuously work on adding more languages to ensure everyone can access legal information in their preferred language.
